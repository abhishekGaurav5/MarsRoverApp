Mars Rover

A Mars rover is a motor vehicle designed to travel on the surface of Mars.
Rovers have several advantages over stationary landers: they examine more territory, they can be directed to interesting features, they can place themselves in sunny positions to weather winter months, and they can advance the knowledge of how to perform very remote robotic vehicle control. They serve a different purpose than orbital spacecraft like Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.

This App uses NASA's Api to fetch the images clicked by three different Rovers. Choose any rover to begin with.
